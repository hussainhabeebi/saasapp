🤝 Human Handover
By default the bot keeps replying even after a lead is handed over to a human (e.g. outside business hours, or while waiting for a rep to pick up) — the lead still shows as handed over either way. Turn this on if you'd rather the bot go fully silent the moment a human takes over, so it can never talk over your team.
🤖 Bot Auto-Reply
Turn this off if you're running your own bot (e.g. a custom n8n workflow) wired to the same WhatsApp inbox and want it to own the replies instead. The CRM keeps working exactly as normal either way — leads, stages, analytics, and handover detection keep updating from the conversation; only this bot's own outgoing WhatsApp messages stop sending.
Conversation Stages
Exact messages the bot sends at each stage — sent only when the customer gives a genuine reply that moves the conversation forward (e.g. "yes", a booking time), never mixed into an answer to a question. Keep these generic ("Would you like to see pricing?") rather than naming a specific product or service — if you sell multiple, the bot already pulls in live details for whichever one the customer is actually asking about when they ask, from your Products/Services data. A stage message naming one specific product will read oddly to a customer asking about a different one.
